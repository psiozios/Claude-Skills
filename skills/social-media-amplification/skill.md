# Social Media Amplification

## Overview
Creates platform-specific social content to promote any content piece, product launch, or announcement. Adapts messaging, format, hashtags, and CTAs for each platform and includes posting schedule recommendations.

## When to Use This Skill
- Launching a new product, feature, or content piece
- Promoting an event or webinar
- Amplifying a blog post or newsletter across social
- Running a coordinated multi-platform campaign

## Process

### Step 1: Source Material
Identify:
- The core piece being promoted
- Target audience per platform
- Primary goal (awareness, traffic, conversion, engagement)
- Campaign duration

### Step 2: Platform Adaptation

**LinkedIn:**
- Professional tone, insight-led
- 1300 chars max before "see more"
- No hashtags in body (3 max at end)
- Best: thought leadership, data, personal stories
- Timing: Tue-Thu, 8-10am or 12-1pm

**Twitter/X:**
- Concise, punchy, conversational
- 280 chars (or thread for depth)
- 1-2 hashtags max
- Best: hot takes, data points, questions
- Timing: Mon-Fri, 9am or 12pm

**Instagram:**
- Visual-first, caption supports image
- Up to 2200 chars (but front-load value)
- 5-10 relevant hashtags
- Best: carousels, behind-the-scenes, quotes
- Timing: Mon/Wed/Fri, 11am-1pm

**Facebook:**
- Conversational, community-oriented
- Longer form OK, but lead with hook
- Minimal hashtags
- Best: stories, questions, video
- Timing: Wed-Fri, 1-4pm

### Step 3: Content Calendar
Generate a posting schedule:
- Launch day: all platforms
- Day 2-3: engagement responses, thread expansion
- Day 4-7: alternative angles, user reactions
- Week 2: results/impact follow-up

## Key Rules
- Never post the same content across all platforms
- Each post must work standalone (don't assume cross-platform viewing)
- Engage with comments within the first hour
- Visual content outperforms text-only on every platform
- Schedule posts but respond to engagement in real-time
