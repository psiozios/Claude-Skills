# Lead Magnet Creator

## Overview
Creates unique lead magnets (PDFs, checklists, mini-courses, templates, calculators) tailored to specific offerings rather than generic content. Maps each lead magnet to a customer journey stage, specific pain points, and conversion goals.

## When to Use This Skill
- Building an email list for a product or service
- Creating a content upgrade for a blog post
- Developing a top-of-funnel asset
- Designing a free resource that qualifies leads

## Process

### Step 1: Strategic Alignment
Define the lead magnet's role:
- **Journey stage**: Awareness, consideration, or decision?
- **Pain point**: What specific problem does it solve?
- **Next step**: What should the reader do after consuming it?
- **Qualification**: How does this attract the RIGHT leads?

### Step 2: Format Selection
Choose based on audience and goal:
- **Checklist**: Quick wins, high completion rate
- **Template**: Practical, saves time, shows expertise
- **Mini-guide**: Deeper value, positions as authority
- **Calculator/Tool**: Interactive, high perceived value
- **Swipe file**: Collection of examples, easy to reference
- **Video training**: Personal connection, complex topics

### Step 3: Content Creation
- Lead with the most valuable insight (don't gate the obvious)
- Include one genuine "aha moment" that proves your expertise
- End with a natural bridge to your paid offering
- Design for consumption in under 15 minutes
- Make it actionable — the reader should DO something

### Step 4: Optimization
- Title promises a specific outcome
- Landing page copy matches the content's actual value
- Delivery email reinforces the value and sets expectations
- Follow-up sequence nurtures toward the next step

## Key Rules
- Solve one specific problem completely, not many problems partially
- The lead magnet should be good enough to charge for
- Always align with what you're eventually selling
- Include your branding but don't make it feel like an ad
- Test the title — it's 80% of conversion
