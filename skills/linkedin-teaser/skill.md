# LinkedIn Teaser

## Overview
Creates LinkedIn promotional posts for articles and content using brand voice and conversion-focused messaging. Structures posts with hooks, value previews, social proof elements, and clear CTAs optimized for LinkedIn's algorithm.

## When to Use This Skill
- Promoting a new blog post or newsletter
- Sharing insights from a recent project
- Announcing a product or feature launch
- Repurposing long-form content for LinkedIn

## Process

### Step 1: Extract Core Value
From the source content, identify:
- The single most compelling insight
- The target audience on LinkedIn
- The desired action (click, comment, share, follow)

### Step 2: Structure the Post

**Line 1-2 (Hook):** Stop the scroll. Options:
- Bold statement or hot take
- Surprising data point
- Personal story opening
- Direct challenge to the reader

**Line 3-8 (Value):**
- Deliver 2-3 key insights from the content
- Use short paragraphs (1-2 sentences)
- Include specific numbers or examples
- Line breaks between each point

**Line 9-10 (CTA):**
- Soft CTA: Ask a question related to the topic
- Hard CTA: Direct link with reason to click
- Engagement CTA: "Agree or disagree?"

### Step 3: Optimize for LinkedIn
- Keep under 1300 characters for "see more" threshold (first 210 chars visible)
- Use line breaks generously
- No more than 3 hashtags (place at end)
- Tag relevant people or companies sparingly
- Avoid external links in the main post (put in comments if possible)

## Key Rules
- The first line must work as a standalone hook
- Give enough value that the post is worth reading even without clicking
- Write as a person, not a brand — LinkedIn rewards authenticity
- One post, one idea — don't try to cover everything
- Test hooks: if it doesn't make YOU want to read more, rewrite it
