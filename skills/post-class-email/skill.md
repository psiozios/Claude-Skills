# Post Class Email

## Overview
Designs conversion-focused follow-up email sequences sent after live events (webinars, workshops, classes, conferences). Covers immediate recap, value reinforcement, social proof, urgency elements, and final CTAs with timing recommendations.

## When to Use This Skill
- After hosting a webinar or workshop
- Following a live demo or training session
- Post-conference speaker follow-up
- Any live event with a conversion goal

## Process

### Email Sequence (5 emails)

**Email 1: Immediate Recap (send within 2 hours)**
- Thank attendees
- Recap top 3 takeaways
- Include replay link (if available)
- One soft CTA

**Email 2: Value Reinforcement (Day 1-2)**
- Share a bonus resource related to the event
- Address common questions from the live session
- Social proof: quote from an attendee or testimonial
- Slightly stronger CTA

**Email 3: Case Study / Proof (Day 3-4)**
- Share a success story from someone who took the next step
- Include specific results and timeline
- Address the #1 objection
- Clear CTA with benefit statement

**Email 4: Urgency / Scarcity (Day 5-6)**
- Deadline reminder (if applicable)
- What they'll miss by not acting
- Final bonus or incentive
- Direct, clear CTA

**Email 5: Last Chance (Day 7)**
- Final reminder
- Simple, short message
- Binary choice: act now or this opportunity closes
- P.S. with the strongest selling point

### Customization
- Adjust timing for your audience (B2B = slower, B2C = faster)
- Segment by attendee vs no-show (different first email)
- Split test subject lines on emails 2 and 3

## Key Rules
- Every email must provide standalone value (not just a sales pitch)
- Subject lines should not all follow the same pattern
- Personalize with the attendee's name and event-specific details
- Include an unsubscribe option and respect it immediately
- Test on mobile — most emails are read on phones
